This Limited Warranty covers any defects in material or workmanship under normal use during the Warranty Period.

This Limited Warranty does not cover any problem that is caused by: ● conditions, malfunctions or damage not resulting from defects in material or workmanship -This includes damage (i.e. Cracked glass, water damage, etc.) as the result of user error.

To obtain warranty service, you must first contact us to determine the problem and the most appropriate solution for you. Client must provide an Owl Fix IT technician with a photo of the damage (if applicable) and the IMEI number of the device (if applicable).
